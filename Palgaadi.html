Overview

Palgaadi is a car pooling service for inter as well as intra city commute. An inexpensive and environment friendly way of getting around.

Process & My Role

I began from understanding current solutions and unaddressed issues to creating high fidelity prototype of the app.

Understand Private Road Transportation

I conducted a secondary research to gain a better insight into the transportation industry and its users. Transportation industry is third largest and fastest growing sectors. Explosive motorization trends are seen in recent years due to urbanisation, at the highest ever growth rate of 34%, and economic growth of the country.
Cars and motorbikes registered in the last 10 years is twice than the registrations in last 54 years. As per the financial report by the GoI (Government of India), transport infrastructure sector will grow by average 5.9 per cent annually through 2021.

Opportunity Gap

Carpooling is the new big thing according to The Verge. Keeping with the trend many startups have sprung here in India as well. Some failed to survive the dynamic markets and everchanging needs of customers but some managed to stay afloat by providing valuable alternatives in the form of peer to peer sharing services.

Understand the User

I conducted contextual interviews with 12 passengers and 9 car owners to understand their pain points and car-pooling habits. Pain points and concerns segragated in different phases of carpooling are -

Interviewed carpoolers, used mostly QuickRide and SRide. Despite of the pain points, users definitely saw value in the idea. Following points of delight came up time and again.

• Users felt good and boasted their contribution to green initiative.
• Car owners enjoyed the company of others, mostly in longer but also shorter routes.
• Passengers acknowledged and appreciated the fact that they are not adding to the traffic, if they had hailed an uber or ola.
• Passengers were extremely satisfied by their quality of travel as compared to cabs. Car owners are polite and their vehicles are clean and maintained.
• Car owners as well as passengers are thrilled about their money savings. Car owners are earning more than the fuel cost and passengers are spending way less than they would on cab services.
• Accustomed users felt safe with other verified users.

Early User Insights

During contextual interviews, an interesting thing surfaced that carpool apps are quite unpopular. And almost all users knew about the apps only by word of mouth.

• Potential users are open to the idea of carpooling, if safety of travel is assured.
• Phone call is the most relied upon method to finalize the route, pickup points and drop locations.
• Car owners and passengers are mostly out of sync in an ongoing ride.
• Users prefer to mark their own route selection.
• Drivers do not prefer to go out of their way to pickup.
• Passengers mostly walk to the agreed pickup location, which is the route taken by driver that day.
• Users are sceptical about passengers/drivers from other companies.
• Unverified users are not preferred by drivers as well as passengers.

Design Principle

Smoothen the experience of intra or inter-city commute in a hassle-free, clean and inexpensive way.

Solution

Unchallenging ride creation

A passenger easily navigates through limited elements on the interface to search for a suitable ride. Pickup and destination fields are pre-filled as the app learns from users' repeated inputs. Search for a returning user is just 1 click action.

Assist the passenger to take better decisions. Sometimes walking is faster than waiting. Presenting user with alternate pickup points within walkable distance that conincide with more car routes, not only increases their chances of match but also significantly reduces any ambiguity about exact pickup locations.

People usually have luggage for inter-city travels. Suggesting luggage quantity and a personal note is more valid for both passenger and driver than walkable alternatives.

Search made easier

Showing each ride on the map with all helpful decision making attributes on top lets user decide a ride of his liking. Clearly showing the last mile walking distance in map and with symbols clarifies the type of journey user is requesting.

Presenting descision making attributes upfront and in a clean interface increases stickiness and likeability of the app. Updating the user with real-time updates on their request further inspires confidence and chances of ride completion.

Transparent ride tracking

Keeping all the passengers updated of the current status of ride like route, other pickups, estimated time of pickup promotes completion rate. Easily accessible and conviniently placed message box lets users communicate with everyone on the ride. This and ride details card keeps everyone on the same page and reduces ambiguity.

Time delays are avoided as system keeps track of users' location and prompts via push notifications to reach the pickup location in time for car arrival. Pickup time and walking time to pickup location is clearly indicated for passengers' convinience.

Car Owners's Experience

Vehicle owners are the back-bone of the serivce. Unintrusive and fluid app experience is critical to grasp their attention.

Vehicle verification is of paramount importance for a boarding passenger. Hence the user is prompted verify the vehicle before creating up a pool-ride. Showcasing system transparency and reason is key in building trust and overcoming discomfort about sharing personal details. After successful verification, vehicle is registered in the users profile and ride is created.

Curate your route

Drivers running errands or with personal prefernces may have their own route that is different from map suggestions. In such cases for better accuracy in matching with the passengers, option to create a personal route is effective.

Selectively choose passengers

Vehicle owners can selectively choose the passengers whose pickup points lie within suitable detour time. Additionl detour time denoted in ride-request and offer-ride reduces cognitive load of the driver resulting in faster decision.

Effortless driving

To make driving effortless, audible instructions are turned on when ride is started. This way drivers' knowledge is always updated with new advancements or alterations. To let the user focus on driving and less on managing the ride, smart navigation is turned which automatically follows ride sequence and navigates the driver to his next location. Driver is timely prompted based on geolocation, to onboard a passenger.
