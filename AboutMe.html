Hi, I am Nancy. Driven by endless curiosity, I want to make digital experiences simple and pleasant. I started my career 3 years ago as a software developer, gradually my job drifted me in the world of design and I am extremly grateful for it. Today I design and build native and cloud softwares. I dove headfirst in the field and have learnt everything by practicing and from my amazing mentors. I like building new products from scratch and optimizing existing ones to perform better in a more human way. Conceptual design and prototyping are the stages I enjoy the most.

When I am not working I am usually cycling around my city, watching an interesting multimedia piece, reading, brush lettering or practicing yoga. I live to travel to new places and I deeply care about the climate change.

Thanks for stopping by!
